I'm an electrical engineer and PhD researcher at National Yunlin University of Science and Technology, Taiwan, and a lecturer at Politeknik Manufaktur Negeri Bangka Belitung, Indonesia.

My research sits at the intersection of biomedical imaging, geometric deep learning, and medical image segmentation & classification — spanning brain tumor MRI, pancreatic cystic lesions, endoscopic grading, and microscopy image analysis, with a focus on symmetry-aware, group-equivariant neural networks for real clinical impact.
